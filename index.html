3) Project Overview and Why It Matters

ClaimSwift AI is an explainable straight-through claims engine that automates more than intake. It validates policy coverage, checks fraud risk, calculates payout, and makes a transparent decision to approve, verify, or escalate. This improves customer speed and insurer control at the same time.

Explainable Decisions

Every outcome includes reason codes such as coverage fit, duplicate risk, and confidence quality.

Fraud-Aware Automation

The system balances speed with leakage control by using anomaly and duplicate checks before settlement.

Policy Guardrails

Rule-based validation for eligibility, exclusions, deductible, and limits keeps outcomes auditable.

Human-in-the-Loop Safety

Risk tiers drive routing so only safe low-risk claims auto-settle while others move to verification.

4) Further Implementation Plan

  1. Phase 1 (MVP): Single claim category, OCR extraction, rules, baseline risk model, and dashboard.
  2. Phase 2 (Pilot): Configurable rules, better parsing, explanation panel, and manual verification queue.
  3. Phase 3 (Operations): Payment workflow integration, stronger duplicate checks, and audit hardening.
  4. Phase 4 (Scale): Multi-product support, enterprise security controls, and advanced fraud intelligence.
